About Study Prep

Study Prep is an initiative of the JOHESSA E-Library created to help Medical Laboratory Science students prepare for CBT examinations through organized practice questions arranged according to academic level. The platform provides students with an interactive learning experience that allows them to assess their understanding, improve their speed, and build confidence before examinations.

Mission

To improve academic excellence through technology-driven examination practice.

Vision

To become the leading online CBT platform for Medical Laboratory Science students.

Objectives

Provide quality practice questions from 100 Level to 500 Level in an engaging learning environment.
