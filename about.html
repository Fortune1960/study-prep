About Study Prep

Welcome NIMELSSITES!! Study Prep is an initiative of the Nimelssa Librarian created to help Medical Laboratory Science students prepare for CBT examinations through organized practice questions arranged according to academic level. This platform provides students with an interactive learning experience that allows them to assess their understanding, improve their speed, and build confidence before examinations. I encourage you to make the most out of this platform, and use it as a tool for success.
